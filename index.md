# Aaron Stewart
{: .page-title }

<p class="subtitle">Ph.D. Student in Mathematics · New Mexico State University</p>

I am a Ph.D. student at New Mexico State University, working under the supervision
of [Prasit Bhattacharya](https://www.prasit.org/). I earned my B.S. in Mathematics
at Western Colorado University and my M.S. in Mathematics at New Mexico State
University, also under the supervision of Prasit Bhattacharya. My master's thesis
presentation was on *operads, $A_n$ spaces and $A_\infty$ spaces*, with an emphasis
on the recognition of loop spaces.

When I am not doing mathematics, I play music and take part in a variety of
endurance sports. My favorite author is Thomas Pynchon — not relevant information
to know, but unavoidable in the sharing.

## Research Interests

I work in homotopy theory and algebraic topology, with a particular interest in
higher categorical algebra. Some specific themes I think about:

- **Operads** and the recognition of iterated loop spaces
- **$A_n$ and $A_\infty$ structures**, including $A_n$ ring spectra
- **Toda brackets** and higher operations in stable homotopy theory
- **Higher categorical algebra**
